Crystal Collector

Can you collect the same amount of crystals as the computer?

Here's how to play the game:


  1. Try to match the random number displayed below at the start of the game ("Number to match") by clicking on the crystals and collecting points.
  2. If you are able to match the "Number to match" by collecting the exact amount of crystal points, you win!
  3. If the amount of crystal points you collect adds up to more than the "Number to", you lose.
  4. Each time the game starts, a new random number is generated, and the values of the crystals change...tricky!
