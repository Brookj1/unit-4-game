Crystal Collector!

Here's how to play the game:


  1. Try to match the random number displayed below at the start of the game ("Number to match") by collecting crystal points.
  2. Each crystal below is worth a certain number of points, which can only be revealed by clicking on it.
  3. Clicking on a crystal will add those points to your total score.
  4. If you are able to match the "Number to match" by collect the exact amount of crystal points, you win!
  5. If the amount of crystal points you collect adds up to more than the "Number to", you lose.
  6. Each time the game starts, a new random number is generated, and the values of the crystals change...tricky!
  7. Good luck and have fun!
